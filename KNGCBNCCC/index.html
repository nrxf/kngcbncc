About Us

Welcome to Kabi Nazrul Government College BNCC contingent. We are proud BNCC cadets dedicated to serving our country through discipline, leadership, and community service.

Short History of BNCC

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) was established to develop a disciplined and patriotic youth. Originally formed under the Indian Territorial Forces Act in 1923, BNCC trains students in military skills, leadership, and social responsibility, preparing them to contribute positively to society and national defense.
